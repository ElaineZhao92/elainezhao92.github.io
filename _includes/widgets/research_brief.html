Research

Foundation models have demonstrated remarkable capabilities, but they are inherently constrained by their black-box nature and a reliance on static training paradigms, which often lead to overconfident yet erroneous outputs. In the high-stakes financial domain, these limitations are further amplified by non-stationary data and extreme market volatility, where the lack of transparency poses catastrophic risks, necessitating rigorous risk management and regulatory compliance.

Motivated by these challenges, my research focuses on building Trustworthy AI and AI4Finance through three core dimensions: uncertainty quantification, explainability to align model outputs with human reasoning, and robustness to ensure stability under distribution shifts.
